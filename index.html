Questions people ask

Is it really free?
Yes. The planner, tracker, water and weight logs are free forever. The optional Premium Pack (₹99 one-time) adds a full 7-day region-matched plan with a grocery list.
Will I have to eat boiled chicken and salads?
No. Plans are built from your region's everyday meals — dal-roti, idli-sambar, macher jhol, thepla. Fat loss comes from portions and consistency, not exotic food.
Where is my data stored?
Only on your own device (browser storage). Nothing is uploaded — we couldn't see your data even if we wanted to.
How fast will I lose weight?
The default deficit targets roughly 0.45 kg/week — the pace most dietitians consider sustainable. Slower is fine; consistency beats speed.
